Mobile App Name: HANME UP

It has established modern learning using technology in sign languages essential to communicate with those with exceptional disabilities, specifically deaf and mute children that lowest range age at 5 years old.

1ST YEAR IN BSIT

Mobile App Name: UNISCI (Universal Science)

Inspired based in the Philippine context, this application is designed to concentrate on teaching children aged 5 and above about science and history in the country. Its goal is to aid their development and to sharpen their minds, fostering creativity and intelligence.

The creator of UNISCI (Universal Science) intends to initiate the process in a straightforward manner and increase their learning.
